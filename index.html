QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Questions teams ask us

How much do corporate wellness programs cost?
Every program is quoted to your team size, location and format. You get a fixed number on a free 30-minute call, no obligation.
What team sizes do you work with?
From intimate circles of 6 up to full floors of 100+. We adjust facilitation, and split or add facilitators for very large groups.
Do you come to our office in Gurgaon / Delhi-NCR?
Yes. On-site across Gurgaon and Delhi-NCR is our most-booked format. We also host at partner studios and run nature retreats nearby.
How often should we run sessions?
One-off resets work, but most teams see the biggest shift with a recurring weekly or monthly slot. Recurring plans cost less per session.
How do you measure impact?
We gather short post-session feedback from participants (the basis of our reported outcomes) and can share a simple summary for your team leads.
Is any of this religious?
No. Everything is secular and inclusive — a nervous-system reset and team-wellbeing practice, nothing devotional.
What do you need from us to run a session?
A suitable room or space and calendar time. We bring bowls, mats, audio and all materials, and arrive early to set up.
How do we get started?
Send the enquiry form or message on WhatsApp. We'll set up a 30-minute discovery call, then shape a custom proposal for your team.
