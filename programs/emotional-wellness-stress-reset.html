THE RESET

For teams carrying pressure, fatigue, and mental noise.

This session creates a calm reset inside the workday. The format blends sound healing, guided breathwork and meditation so people can settle their nervous system and leave with repeatable tools.

INSIDE THE SESSION
  • Guided sound healing with Tibetan bowls
  • Breathwork & pranayama sessions
  • Mindfulness & body-scan meditation
  • Stress-mapping workshop
  • Take-home practice toolkit
SESSION RHYTHM

Quiet structure, not a rushed workshop.

The experience is paced like a nervous-system reset: soft arrival, guided release, and a grounded close that turns the session into something people can carry back to work.

QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Questions teams ask us

How large a team can join one session?
10–80 people works well. Above 80 we split into two sittings or add a co-facilitator so the sound still reaches everyone.
Our team is sceptical about sound healing. Will this feel awkward?
We keep it grounded — no chanting or forced sharing. It's guided breath, Tibetan bowls and rest. Sceptics usually leave the calmest.
On-site or at a studio?
Both. We travel across Gurgaon and Delhi-NCR to your office, or host at a partner studio. On-site is most popular for full teams.
Is it tied to any religion?
No. It's a secular, nervous-system reset. Nothing devotional, nothing you need to believe in.
How much space and setup do you need?
A room where people can sit or lie down. We bring bowls, mats and audio. You provide the room and 45 min of setup access.
Can remote or hybrid teams do this?
Yes — we run a guided online version for distributed teams, though on-site lands deeper.
What does it cost?
Quoted to your team size, location and format. Book a call for a fixed number.
How do we book and what's the notice?
Message on WhatsApp or send the form. We usually need 5–7 days' notice; rush dates possible.
