THE RESET

For teams carrying pressure, fatigue, and mental noise.

This session creates a calm reset inside the workday. The format blends sound healing, guided breathwork and meditation so people can settle their nervous system and leave with repeatable tools.

INSIDE THE SESSION
  • Guided sound healing with Tibetan bowls
  • Breathwork & pranayama sessions
  • Mindfulness & body-scan meditation
  • Stress-mapping workshop
  • Take-home practice toolkit
SESSION RHYTHM

Quiet structure, not a rushed workshop.

The experience is paced like a nervous-system reset: soft arrival, guided release, and a grounded close that turns the session into something people can carry back to work.

01

Settle

Breathwork and body awareness bring the team out of work-mode and into a calmer shared room.

02

Release

Tibetan bowls, guided meditation and emotional mapping help clear built-up stress without forcing discussion.

03

Integrate

Simple take-home practices make the reset practical for desks, meetings and high-pressure days.

DURATION
2–4 Hours
FORMAT
On-site / Studio
TEAM SIZE
10–80 People
