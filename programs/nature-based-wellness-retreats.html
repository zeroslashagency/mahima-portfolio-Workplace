THE RETREAT

For teams that need distance from routine and deeper reconnection.

This retreat format creates space for recovery, bonding and reflection. It works well for leadership groups, culture resets and teams carrying prolonged work intensity.

INSIDE THE RETREAT

QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Questions teams ask us

Where do the retreats happen?
At nature venues within reach of Gurgaon/Delhi-NCR. We match the site to your group size and budget.
How long are they?
Half-day, full-day or up to two days with overnight stay. Longer formats go deeper on bonding.
What group size works?
8–30. Small enough for real sharing circles, big enough for team energy.
Do you handle logistics and meals?
We coordinate flow, facilitation and sattvic meals; travel/stay is arranged with you or your admin team.
What activities are included?
Forest bathing, outdoor sound healing, sunrise yoga, breathwork and team sharing circles.
Is it physically demanding?
No — gentle, restorative pacing. Walks are easy and optional.
What does it cost?
Quoted per head depending on venue, nights and group size. Get a tailored number.
How far ahead should we plan?
2–3 weeks minimum so venue and logistics lock in cleanly.
