THE CIRCLE

For women who need a grounded space to share, release and rise.

This circle is designed to support emotional honesty, confidence and workplace belonging. The tone is warm, contained and practical, with facilitation that respects privacy and group comfort.

INSIDE THE CIRCLE
  • Facilitated sharing & listening circles
  • Emotional release & somatic exercises
  • Confidence & self-worth practices
  • Women's leadership discussion
  • Closing ritual & intention-setting
CIRCLE RHYTHM

Safety first, then sharing.

The circle is designed to feel held and respectful. It moves slowly from trust-building into guided expression, then closes with confidence and personal intention.

01

Ground

Simple rituals and listening agreements create a calm, private container for the group.

02

Share

Facilitated prompts and somatic exercises support release without pressure to perform.

03

Rise

Confidence practices and intentions turn the circle into grounded workplace support.

DURATION
2–3 Hours
FORMAT
Women Only
TEAM SIZE
6–25 People
