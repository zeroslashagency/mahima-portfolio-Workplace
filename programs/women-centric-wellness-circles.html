THE CIRCLE

For women who need a grounded space to share, release and rise.

This circle is designed to support emotional honesty, confidence and workplace belonging. The tone is warm, contained and practical, with facilitation that respects privacy and group comfort.

INSIDE THE CIRCLE
  • Facilitated sharing & listening circles
  • Emotional release & somatic exercises
  • Confidence & self-worth practices
  • Women's leadership discussion
  • Closing ritual & intention-setting
CIRCLE RHYTHM

Safety first, then sharing.

The circle is designed to feel held and respectful. It moves slowly from trust-building into guided expression, then closes with confidence and personal intention.

01

Ground

Simple rituals and listening agreements create a calm, private container for the group.

02

Share

Facilitated prompts and somatic exercises support release without pressure to perform.

03

Rise

Confidence practices and intentions turn the circle into grounded workplace support.

QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Questions teams ask us

Who is this for?
Women's networks, ERGs and teams — from ICs to leadership. Built around trust and honest sharing.
Is sharing mandatory?
Never. The circle invites sharing but listening is always enough. No one is put on the spot.
How many can join a circle?
6–25. Smaller keeps it intimate and safe; we split larger groups into circles.
Is confidentiality protected?
Yes — we open every circle with a confidentiality agreement so people feel safe to be honest.
On-site or studio?
Either — a private room at your office or a calm studio in Gurgaon/NCR.
Can we run this for Women's Day?
Yes, it's a popular Women's Day and IWD-week booking. Reserve early — those dates fill fast.
What does it cost?
Quoted per circle, scaled to size and duration. Book a call for a number.
How much notice do you need?
5–7 days typically; more for Women's Day week.
