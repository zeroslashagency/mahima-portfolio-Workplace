THE RELEASE

For teams that sit too long and carry stress in the body.

This session brings movement back into the work culture. The flow is beginner-friendly, practical and designed to release tension without making people feel exposed or out of place.

INSIDE THE SESSION
  • Corporate yoga (beginner-friendly)
  • Dance therapy & free movement
  • Guided stretching & mobility routines
  • Desk-yoga micro-sessions
  • Relaxation & Yoga Nidra cool-down
SESSION RHYTHM

Body first, mind follows.

This session keeps movement accessible and grounded. The sequence opens gently, builds energy without pressure, and closes with relaxation so people return to work lighter.

01

Mobilise

Simple stretching and breath-led warmups release stiffness from desk posture and long meetings.

02

Express

Gentle yoga or free movement helps the group move energy without performance pressure.

03

Restore

Relaxation and Yoga Nidra settle the body before the team returns to the workday.

QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Questions teams ask us

Do people need to be fit or flexible?
No. Everything is beginner-friendly and scalable — desk-yoga to gentle flow. People modify at their own level.
How many can join?
10–100. Big groups work outdoors or in a hall; we bring extra facilitation for large counts.
Indoor or outdoor?
Either. On-site indoors, your terrace/lawn, or a studio. Outdoor sunrise sessions are a favourite.
Can it be a recurring slot?
Yes — many teams book weekly or fortnightly. Recurring packages are cheaper per session.
What should the team wear?
Comfortable, movement-friendly clothing. No special gear needed.
What space and equipment do you need?
Cleared floor space; we provide mats and any props on request.
What does it cost?
Quoted per session, lower on recurring plans. Book a call for your number.
How much notice to book?
5–7 days for one-offs; recurring slots are scheduled up front.
