THE RELEASE

For teams that sit too long and carry stress in the body.

This session brings movement back into the work culture. The flow is beginner-friendly, practical and designed to release tension without making people feel exposed or out of place.

INSIDE THE SESSION
  • Corporate yoga (beginner-friendly)
  • Dance therapy & free movement
  • Guided stretching & mobility routines
  • Desk-yoga micro-sessions
  • Relaxation & Yoga Nidra cool-down
SESSION RHYTHM

Body first, mind follows.

This session keeps movement accessible and grounded. The sequence opens gently, builds energy without pressure, and closes with relaxation so people return to work lighter.

01

Mobilise

Simple stretching and breath-led warmups release stiffness from desk posture and long meetings.

02

Express

Gentle yoga or free movement helps the group move energy without performance pressure.

03

Restore

Relaxation and Yoga Nidra settle the body before the team returns to the workday.

DURATION
1–3 Hours
FORMAT
On-site / Outdoor
TEAM SIZE
10–100 People
