QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Questions teams ask us

Is this a lecture or hands-on?
Hands-on. Journaling, vision-boarding and goal frameworks — people leave with a written action plan, not just notes.
How big can the group be?
8–40 works best for depth. Larger groups lose the personal reflection, so we cap or split.
Will this actually change behaviour or just feel good on the day?
We build accountability frameworks and a 30-day action plan so it carries past the session.
Can you tailor it to managers vs individual contributors?
Yes — we adjust the goal-setting and inner-critic work to the seniority mix you tell us about.
On-site or studio?
Usually on-site as a workshop; studio works for smaller cohorts. We cover Gurgaon and Delhi-NCR.
What space do we need?
A workshop-style room where people can sit in a circle or pods and write.
What does it cost?
Quoted to your group size and duration. Get a fixed number on a quick call.
How far ahead should we book?
5–7 days is comfortable; we can move faster for urgent offsites.
